About me

Gerhard Treiber

Born in Pörtschach am Wörthersee (Austria) where I grew up for the first 15 years, then we moved to Obertauern in the Alps in Salzburg (Austria) where I spent 25 years working in tourism.


Married to a US Girl "WE" decided to move to the US. Taking that opportunity I decided to make a career change and started focusing on web design.


In summer 2014 I completed the Web Design Certificate Program at NOVA Arlington and volunteered as a teacher assistant to gain further experience working for Teri Murphy.


I also used that opportunity to continue my education with the Web Development Certificate Program at NOVA Woodbridge.
